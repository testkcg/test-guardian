Many industries. Countless products. One focus.

At first glance, Koch companies might look curiously unrelated. After all, they stretch from the fibers of fashion to the rugged world of ranching. When you look deeper, there’s a common thread that ties them together. It’s a desire to keep improving the products that meet your basic needs every single day.

  • Historically 90% of earnings reinvested into the company
