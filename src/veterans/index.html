Our mission is your success.

As a proud Military Friendly® employer, we created the Koch Vets program to guide veterans or transitioning service men and women to greater success.

Employer Support of the Guard and Reserve, a committee of the Department of Defense, has recognized Koch companies with a total of six awards for providing a supportive workplace for employees who serve in the military.
