Minerals aren’t just part of your daily multivitamin. They are crucial to our daily way of life. From chemistry to manufacturing to power generation, customers worldwide require a reliable, high-quality supply. That’s where we come in.

Together with our subsidiaries, we market, trade and manage logistics for millions of tons of product every year – including petroleum coke, coal, sulfur, wood pulp, paper products and other vital substances. We’re also engaged in oil and gas exploration and production, oil field products, steel and more.
