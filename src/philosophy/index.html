At the heart of how we work.

At the foundation of our approach is Market-Based Management®.

Developed by Charles Koch, MBM® prepares individuals to deal successfully with the challenges of growth and change while fostering a healthy, growing organization. By applying the same shared values that unleash prosperity in free societies – such as value creation, integrity, responsibility, free speech and tolerance – we encourage each other to take initiative and to challenge.

Ten principles.
One proven approach.

Market-Based Management is a way for organizations to succeed by helping others improve their lives. It is the business philosophy and framework that we apply to innovate, improve and transform ourselves in order to create greater value and have fulfillment. Our MBM Guiding Principles are who we are as a company and they guide everything we do.

Change isn't the enemy.
It's your next opportunity.

In 1961, Koch was valued at $21 million. By 2014, $100 billion.

How did one organization create such dramatic, long-term growth? Especially in light of countless other companies that failed to adapt and ceased to exist?

We believe the answers to our success lie in understanding the very nature of change and finding the courage to fully embrace it. In doing so, we develop the foresight to anticipate change. Occasionally we even help drive it by challenging basic assumptions and each other to discover new and better ways to create value for others.
