Grant Inquiries

Does my organization qualify?

To be considered for a Koch grant, you must:

  • Be a non-profit organization, as defined in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code
  • Share our focus on enabling youth, environmental stewardship and education
  • Have clearly defined parameters and measurable results

In keeping with these requirements, we do not support:

  • Individuals or for-profit organizations
  • Health-related causes
  • Fundraising events, endowments, team or trip sponsorships, capital campaigns, contests or advertising
